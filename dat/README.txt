The land-use history dataset used in these scripts is currently unable to be uploaded, but will be updated as possible.

The long-form dataset (230720_histLong.csv) contains three columns: 

PointID - the ID of the site as used throughout all datasets
year - year of reported land-use; continuous, year-by-year, from oldest known land-use to sampling date
use - the land use for the given year and site
